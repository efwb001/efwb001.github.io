Summary

Hi! I'm a postdoc in Richard Granger's Brain Engineering Laboratory at Dartmouth. I study how the brain learns to compute - what algorithms the brain implements, what architecture enables them, and how they arise.

I received my PhD in cognitive neuroscience from Dartmouth, my MS in machine learning from UC Irvine, and my BS in computer engineering from Bucknell.
